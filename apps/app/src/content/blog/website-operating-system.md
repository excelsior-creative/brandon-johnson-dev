The next useful website is less like a brochure and more like an operating surface.

For many teams, the site is where prospects arrive, content gets published, forms create work, analytics reveal demand, and internal systems need clean context. When those pieces stay disconnected, the team pays for it in manual follow-up.

Static delivery can still be the right foundation. The trick is moving dynamic operational work to focused integrations while keeping the public site fast, crawlable, and simple to maintain.
